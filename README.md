##  1. Scenario. "Search" button functionalities

 2. Click on "Search" button on the wright side ;

 4. Click on "Search" button;
 5. Check if the result is "Juodi degtukai";
 
 ## 2. Scenario. "Cart" functionalities
### 2. Adding product to the "Cart"
 1. Open website "https://monu.lt/"; 
 2. Scroll down to the products;  
 3. Click on first product "Þalioji arbata Teministeriet – 262, braðkës ir avietës";
 4. Click on "Á krepðelá";
 5. Open Cart and check if the product is in the cart.

 ## 2. Scenario. "Cart" functionalities

 ## 3. Scenario. "Kontaktai" functionalities
### 5. Checking sending message possibility   
 1. Open website "https://monu.lt/"; 
 2. Scroll down to the end of website;  
 3. Click on "Kontaktai";
 4. Scroll to the end of the website;
 5. Enter your name in the "Uþpildykite kontaktø formà/Jûsø vardas";
 6. Enter your email in the "Uþpildykite kontaktø formà/Jûsø elektroninio paðto adresas";
 7. Enter the theme in the "Uþpildykite kontaktø formà/Jûsø tema";
 8. Enter your message in the "Uþpildykite kontaktø formà/Jûsø þinutë";
 9. Check if the message is sent.
